# Ambience Audio Sources

The reader ambience player is wired for real audio recordings. Direct downloads from Wikimedia were rate-limited during setup, so the app currently streams these source files directly.

- Rain: "Sound of rain.ogg" by Effib, licensed CC BY-SA 3.0.
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Sound_of_rain.ogg
- Fireplace: "Campfire sound ambience.ogg" by Glaneur de sons, licensed CC BY 3.0.
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Campfire_sound_ambience.ogg
- Page turn: "Turning a page.ogg" by planish, public domain.
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Turning_a_page.ogg
